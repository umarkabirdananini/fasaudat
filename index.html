About Us

The Fasaudat Support Foundation was established in 2017, the Foundation is Nonprofit Humanitarian Assistances Organization based at Sokoto. It main objectives provision of philanthropic charitable and social well being services to the orphans, less privileges and needy ones in the society, hospitals, schools etc. Since the organization has been expanding continuously in terms of sustainable and promoting , equitable and encourage development of education, health care services, social welfare and other service through the social research dissemination and welfare policy.

Since its establishment it has accomplished the following major activities and projects among others:

(a)Sponsor more than 500 students vulnerable children and orphans in several schools and institutions.

(b)Provide school materials such as complete school uniforms, sandals, branded exercise books and writing materials to (list the names of this schools, Government Day Achida, Government Day secondary School Wurno, Government Day Secondary School Gagi, Birkini Model Primary School Jabbo and Government Girls Secondary School Jabbo, Fasaudat Support Foundation gave school items too.

(c)Donated complete school uniforms, toiletries and food items to the Orphanage Home Government Road, Sokoto.

(d)Donated drugs and medical sunshine’s as well as sponsoring surgerical operations in Maryam Abacha Specialist Hospital.

(e) Intended to donated school uniforms and other items to Abshirul Ummah Islamiyya Model Primary School Mainiyo Sokoto North Local Government Area.

Beliefs

  • Freedom of all to work, education and social development especially the orphans and less privilege ones.
  • Equal opportunity for all orphans and individual less privileged ones in society irrespective of class, age, ethnic, culture or religious race etc.
  • Human right for all.
  • All orphans should be giving equal opportunity in term of social welfare in the society.
  • Empowerment of the poor and rural people to be given appropriate health care, education in our society.

For the past years we have been working in field of:

  • Working for the promotion of the values education in our community.
  • Promoting health care system.
  • Social welfare through the visitation of the foundation to the orphanage homes, also in the state by donating food, items, cloths and education equipment etc.
  • Social development of underprivileged individuals and groups and communities.
  • Entrepreneurship skills to be self-reliant for both youth and women.
  • Peaceful coexistence and self-reliance through implementation of sustainable programs for a better future for orphans and widows.
  • Education Development
  • Health Care Development
  • Improvement of Girls Child Education

Fatima Ahmad Maigari

CEO, Fasaudat Support Foundation

Agri-preneur and also a philanthropist, known for founding Fasaudat Support Foundation (FSF) and serves as its Chairlady, Chief Executive Officer.

About the Founder

In addition to the charity work she has been doing,
Fatima Maigari is also known as a Farmer Entrepreneur Business, buying and selling livestock rearing etc. Maigari grow up seeing her mother daily involved in service to the community, neighbours and even people from a far, her mother was never a person that will turn away find something no matter how little its, she gives from her own small resources.

She also grow up with several abounded orphans that her mother taking care of after the demise of their parent or those that abandoned. It is of no surprise at all her after growing up that find herself being drawn to offering all kinds of assistance to people whenever she find them. She find herself emotionally drawn to orphans, less priviledge and needy generally, that is why she is currently involved almost full-time into this charity work of providing and improving the lives of orphans, less priviledge and needy with a better life in our society. 

Despite the fact that she is not rich person and the work involved a lot of resource and finance, she use the little profit from her business, with the assistance, she regularly received from her parent donation in different kinds and forms towards the success of this charitable work.
