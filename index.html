About Us

The Fasaudat Support Foundation was established in 2017, the Foundation is Nonprofit Humanitarian Assistances Organization based at Sokoto. It main objectives provision of philanthropic charitable and social well being services to the orphans, less privileges and needy ones in the society, hospitals, schools etc. Since the organization has been expanding continuously in terms of sustainable and promoting , equitable and encourage development of education, health care services, social welfare and other service through the social research dissemination and welfare policy.

Since its establishment it has accomplished the following major activities and projects among others:

(a)Sponsor more than 5000 students vulnerable children and orphans in several schools and institutions.

(b)Provide school materials such as complete school uniforms, sandals, branded exercise books and writing materials to (list the names of this schools, Government Day Achida, Government Day secondary School Wurno, Government Day Secondary School Gagi, Birkini Model Primary School Jabbo and Government Girls Secondary School Jabbo, Fasaudat Support Foundation gave school items too.

Vision

Making the orphans and less privileges productive members of society through the provision of social well being services.

Mission

Provision of homes, schools and appropriate project for orphans and less privilege in the society.

Aims

To improve lives of vulnerable youths and women for a better future

To ensure peaceful coexistence and self-reliance through of sustainable programs for a better future for orphans, less privileges ones. 

To work for the social development of under privilege individuals, group and community health programs.

To support women and youths with entrepreneurship skills to be self-reliant.

To promote and improve girls child education most specially in northern path of Nigeria.

Agri-preneur and also a philanthropist, known for founding Fasaudat Support Foundation (FSF) and serves as its Chairlady, Chief Executive Officer.

About the Founder

Hajiya Fatima Ahmed was born on 10th October, 1984 in the ancient city of Kaduna, Rigaehukun Local Government Of Kaduna State, from the family of Late Alhaji Ahmed Maigari.
Hajiya Fatima Ahmed obtained her Primary Certificate from Jalo Model Primary School Regachukun, Olevel Certificate from Government Day Secondary School Rigachukun, and later obtained Certificate in Catering from Mariam Catering School Kano State.

Fatima Maigari is also known as a Farmer Entrepreneur Business, buying and selling livestock rearing etc. Maigari grow up seeing her mother daily involved in service to the community, neighbours and even people from a far, her mother was never a person that will turn away find something no matter how little its, she gives from her own small resources.

She also grow up with several abounded orphans that her mother taking care of after the demise of their parent or those that abandoned. It is of no surprise at all her after growing up that find herself being drawn to offering all kinds of assistance to people whenever she find them. She find herself emotionally drawn to orphans, less privileged and needy generally, that is why she is currently involved almost full-time into this charity work of providing and improving the lives of orphans, less privileged and needy with a better life in our society.

Despite the fact that she is not rich person and the work involved a lot of resource and finance, she use the little profit from her business, with the assistance, she regularly received from her parent donation in different kinds and forms towards the success of this charitable work.
